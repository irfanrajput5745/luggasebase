About Us

About Us

Here at Luggage Base, we're proud to deliver great customer care and the best prices on quality luggage, bags and accessories. Located on California's beautiful Central Coast, we've been a family-owned business since 1971 and the original online luggage retailer since 1996. In addition to our online store, you can find the same great selection at our retail store on Highway 101 in Nipomo, CA.

Luggage Base does not carry every brand of luggage or bags. Instead, we strive to select the best top brands that provide the highest quality luggage in the industry providing great value for our customers. This means that instead of being loyal to any one manufacturer, we're constantly evaluating our brands and our inventory to meet the ever-changing demands of our customers. Shoppers can find great specials on closeouts and special buys as well. Some popular luggage brands include Osprey, Samsonite, Eagle Creek, Kipling, Patagonia, The North Face, Oakley, Travelpro, SwissGear, Delsey, Zero Halliburton, Thule, Granite Gear, baggallini, Piel Leather, Hartmann, Briggs & Riley and many more.

Luggage Base is also here to give you expert advice so that you can make an informed purchase. We offer useful resources like: Buying Luggage Tips, Packing Tips, Luggage Care, and even free Luggage Repair Tips.

We hope you have a great experience shopping with us, either online or in our brick-and-mortar store. We are registered with the Better Business Bureau's online reliability program, Trustwave, Digicert, BizRate. LuggageBase.com is an award winning website including a W3 Award. If you do not find what you're looking for, or you need additional help, please feel free to call our luggage experts during customer care hours.

Our offices and showroom are located at 670 South Frontage Road, Nipomo, CA 93444 (visible from Highway 101, 1 mile south of the Tefft Street exit). Scroll down to see a map!

If you have any questions, please e-mail us at service@luggagebase.com, or call us and talk to a real person at 805-929-8191

Customer Care Hours: M-F 10:00am - 5:00pm Pacific Time
